---
title: Cloud vs Self Hosting
description: Compare Aurogurukul Cloud and self-hosting before choosing your setup.
---

Aurogurukul gives you two ways to run the platform:

- use the hosted version at [Aurogurukul.app](https://aurogurukul.app)
- self-host the open-source stack on your own infrastructure

## Choose Aurogurukul Cloud if

- you want the fastest path to launch
- you do not want to maintain servers, updates, backups, and uptime
- you want the Aurogurukul team to manage the infrastructure for you
- you prefer a predictable subscription over operating your own stack

## Choose self-hosting if

- you want complete control over your data and infrastructure
- you need to run Aurogurukul behind a firewall or inside a private environment
- you want to customize deployment and operations around your own stack
- you are comfortable managing hosting, updates, and maintenance

## At a glance

| Area                   | Aurogurukul Cloud      | Self-hosting                  |
| ---------------------- | -------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Setup time             | Fastest              | Slower, depends on your infra |
| Maintenance            | Managed by Aurogurukul | Managed by you                |
| Infrastructure control | Limited              | Full control                  |
| Data ownership         | Managed service      | Your environment              |
| Operational effort     | Low                  | Higher                        |

## Next steps

If you want a managed setup, visit [Aurogurukul.app](https://aurogurukul.app).

If you want full control, continue with the [self-hosting guide](/self-hosting/introduction).
