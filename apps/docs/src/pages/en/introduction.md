## What is Aurogurukul?

Aurogurukul is a platform for building and selling digital products—courses, memberships, and downloads—together with a site builder, blog, commerce integrations, and reporting.

## Features

See the [features overview](/en/features).

## How you can run it

**Hosted:** The service is available at [aurogurukul.app](https://aurogurukul.app) when you use the managed environment.

**On your infrastructure:** You can deploy the application on servers you control. See [Self hosting](/en/self-hosting/introduction).
