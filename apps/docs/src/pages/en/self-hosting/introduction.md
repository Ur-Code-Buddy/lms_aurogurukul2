This section covers running Aurogurukul on infrastructure you control.

## When to self-host

Consider your own deployment if you:

- need full control of data, networking, and backups
- must keep traffic inside a private network or behind a corporate perimeter

## Deploying the stack

![Self hosting options](/assets/schools/self-host.svg)

Follow the [self hosting guide](/en/self-hosting/self-host) for containers, environment variables, and related services.

## Managed environment

If you use the managed offering instead, it is available at [Aurogurukul.app](https://aurogurukul.app).
